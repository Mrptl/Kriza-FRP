Our Profile

25+ Years of Engineering Legacy & Composite Mastery

KRIZA FRP PRODUCTS is a premier manufacturer specializing in high-performance Fiberglass Reinforced Polymer (FRP/GRP) composite solutions. We design, engineer, and manufacture structurally superior products tailored to withstand the most corrosive, heavy-duty industrial and offshore environments.

From initial conceptual drawing and rigorous mechanical load engineering to automated pultrusion and turnkey site installation, we deliver absolute compliance with international ASTM, NEMA, ASME, and OSHA standards.
