Advanced Manufacturing Techniques

We utilize state-of-the-art processes tailored to the specific mechanical and chemical requirements of each project, ensuring optimal performance and longevity.

engineering

Hand Lay-up (Contact Molding)

The fundamental process for creating highly customized, complex shapes. Layers of fiberglass reinforcement are manually placed into a mold and saturated with catalyzed resin. This technique allows for superior control over laminate thickness and glass-to-resin ratios, ideal for specialized chemical tanks, custom fittings, and large structural components.

  • check_circle Ultimate customizability for complex geometries
  • check_circle High strength-to-weight ratio
  • check_circle Excellent corrosion resistance optimization
cyclone

Filament Winding

A highly automated process where continuous resin-impregnated rovings are wound onto a rotating mandrel. This creates structures with exceptional hoop strength.

Piping Systems Pressure Vessels Storage Tanks
format_shapes

Pultrusion

A continuous manufacturing process producing FRP profiles with constant cross-sections. Continuous fiberglass rovings and mats are pulled through a resin bath and then through a heated forming die. This results in incredibly strong, rigid, and lightweight structural components that serve as superior alternatives to steel or aluminum.
